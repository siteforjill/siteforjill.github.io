For my Bat Mitzvah project, I have chosen to fundraise for HaitiChildren. In 2010, after the devastating earthquake that struck Haiti, I was affected by how many children immediately became orphans. I wanted to help so I decided to sell my used toys that were around my house in the local parks. I did this for several years hoping to help make a difference. It seems natural to me to champion this cause now for my mitzvah project. I chose this organization because these children are so highly challenged and need basics such as food, medical care, and the love from a parent they no longer have. I felt that in order to take on this project, I would need to feel empathy for these children. I have felt what I imagine they feel: sad and lonely, and I realized that I could help them. These children have endured so much; it would be a Mitzvah if I can help them smile and let them know that others are thinking of them.

I am debuting my new song “I’m Broken” in the hope of raising monies for these children. Please download it now and help me help HaitiChildren smile.
